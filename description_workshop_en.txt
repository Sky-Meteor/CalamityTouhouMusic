CalamityTouhouMusic

[h1] Intro [/h1]
This music pack plans to implement (including but not limited to) Touhou remix of all tracks in the official Calamity music pack, which is still being updated.
-
The tracks used in the music pack are recreation Doujin of Touhou, and complies with relevant laws and regulations, including the Touhou project guidelines.

[h1] Credits [/h1]
